Our Programs

Empowering Communities Through Action

Discover our flagship programs driving climate action and youth empowerment across Bangladesh and beyond.

Building a Sustainable Future Together

Our programs are designed to address the climate crisis through education, advocacy, and community engagement. We work across 51 districts in Bangladesh, mobilizing youth and communities to take meaningful climate action.

Featured Programs

Flagship

Youth Cup 2025

Bangladesh Youth COP is a youth-led initiative addressing the climate crisis by assessing local vulnerabilities. Through assessment & youth consultation, we aim to create a 'Youth Position for COP' reflecting the needs of climate affected communities.

Our Program Areas

Climate Justice & Just Transition

Advocating for climate justice and supporting communities in transitioning to sustainable practices. Active participation in COP30 and international climate negotiations.

  • Policy Advocacy
  • International Representation
  • Community Support

Climate Education

Developing climate education materials in Bengali, including the "Gohorotno" comic book distributed to 25+ schools across Bangladesh.

  • Educational Materials
  • School Programs
  • Awareness Campaigns

Youth Mobilization

Training and empowering young climate activists through fellowships, including our Climate Policy Negotiation Fellowship program.

  • Leadership Training
  • Fellowship Programs
  • Network Building

Environmental Justice & Resilience

Building environmental resilience in communities and advocating for environmental justice at grassroots and policy levels.

  • Community Resilience
  • Grassroots Advocacy
  • Sustainable Solutions
