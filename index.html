Founder

Saidur Rahman Siam

Founder & Director | Youth Climate Activist

Founded Brighters Foundation Bangladesh with a vision to empower youth for climate justice and sustainable development.

Leading Bangladesh's youth climate movement and representing the country's young voices at international climate conferences including COP29 and COP30.

Dedicated to mobilizing youth across Bangladesh for climate action and building resilient communities through grassroots initiatives.

Chair

Fariha S Aumi

Climate Advocate | Story Teller | Facilitator | Traveller

Chairs the board with expertise in sustainable development policy and international relations.

Director

Mohammad Shohanur Amin
